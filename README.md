# 🏠 Airbnb Analytics: Multi-Classification & Insight Discovery Using IBM Granite AI via Replicate

### Capstone Project – Student Development Initiative

This project provides an in-depth analysis of the Airbnb Short-Term Rental (STR) market in Los Angeles using both quantitative analysis and AI-assisted insight generation. The goal is to help property owners maximize revenue through data-driven investment, operational, and marketing strategies.

---

## AI Support

This project utilizes **IBM Granite 3.3-8B**, accessed through the Replicate API and integrated with a LangChain Pandas Agent.

### AI-Powered Analytics
- Natural language data exploration
- Automated insight generation
- Host classification and segmentation
- Rapid execution of complex analytical queries

---

## Dataset

- **Dataset:** Los Angeles Airbnb Listings Data (June 2025)
- **Total Records:** 32,442 Listings
- **Source:** Airbnb Open Data
- **Kaggle Notebook:** https://www.kaggle.com/code/nabilahulwana/notebookb602b2655b

---

## Key Findings

### Market Pricing
- Average listing price: **$230.31**
- Median listing price: **$155.00**
- The large gap between mean and median indicates a significant number of premium listings.

### Premium Locations
- **Bel-Air** recorded the highest average nightly rate at **$1,355.57**.

### Competitive Pricing Segment
- Approximately **50% of listings** are concentrated within the **$107–$228** price range, creating a highly competitive market segment.

### High Occupancy Listings
- **9,882 listings** achieved occupancy rates above **70%**.
- Most were managed by hosts classified as **Elite** and **Professional** by the IBM Granite classification model.

---

## Recommendations

### Investment Strategy
Focus on **Entire Home/Apt** properties located in:
- Premium areas such as **Bel-Air** and **Malibu**
- High-demand markets such as **Long Beach**

### Operational Strategy
- Maintain **Superhost** status
- Improve response rates and guest communication
- Adopt characteristics commonly found among **Elite Hosts**

### AI-Driven Marketing
Leverage IBM Granite classification insights to optimize listing descriptions and target profitable customer segments such as:
- Business Travelers
- Long-Stay Guests
- Premium Experience Seekers
